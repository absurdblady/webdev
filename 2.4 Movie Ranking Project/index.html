The Best Movies According to Pawan

My top 3 movies of all time are

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty (2013)

For me, it’s pure courage on screen. A reminder that daydreams are not escapes but invitations to live bolder.

Interstellar

To me, it feels like a love letter stretched across galaxies. Science makes it vast, but love makes it human.

La La Land

I see it as a story of sparks that don’t last forever, yet still light up the rest of our lives.
